What is Pop it?

Pop it literally as "pop it". This is a child's game, the process in which can be compared to the bursting of an air-bubble packing film. But unlike the film, pop-it can be pressed with characteristic sensations and sound "endlessly": dozens of silicone bubble are simply squeezed out in the other direction.

Rules of the game!

The players take turns and click on the Pop-it. It is necessary to finish the row that you started. You can press a maximum of 3 Pop-it at the same time (they must be nearby, you can not press several Pop-it at once if they are not side by side) The task is to make the opponent go last. Who went last - he lost)
